One of the best place to go with family and friends. Visawa garden is located near yeshwant college in vishnu Nagar Nanded.

Nana-Nani Park

This park is located in the old Shivaji Nagar industrial area which is now silent zone and few industries are their. Very good maintained, few people visited the park. If management arranged toys to the kids local grand parents comes their with their family members and enjoy.
